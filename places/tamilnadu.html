Top Tourist Places in Tamil Nadu (A-Z)

Kanyakumari

Best Season: October–March

The southernmost tip of mainland India, famous for sunset & sunrise over three oceans and the Vivekananda Rock Memorial.

Madurai

Best Season: October–March

Ancient city built around the towering, multi-colored gateway towers (gopurams) of Meenakshi Amman Temple.

Mahabalipuram

Best Season: October–March

UNESCO World Heritage site known for seventh-century rock-cut monolithic sanctuaries, Shore Temple, and stone relief carvings.

Ooty

Best Season: September–June

The Queen of Hill Stations in the Nilgiri hills, featuring heritage toy train rides and green botanical gardens.

Rameshwaram

Best Season: October–April

Sacred island town connected by the iconic Pamban Bridge, featuring pristine sandy shores and historic temples.

Best Season: October–March

Capital of Chola architecture, home to the spectacular Great Living Chola temples and unique Thanjavur doll painting arts.
